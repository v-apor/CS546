Disappointed by AI

In the futuristic year of 2025, modern AI systems took over. Moosek posted a poll on twitter asking if he should buy everything. And as expected, the poll resulted in a big yes.

He ended up making a deal with the leading AI firm "MegaHard: ClosedAI"). Now he control over all the AI systems in the world, but he was still disappointed because all of them were bots.
